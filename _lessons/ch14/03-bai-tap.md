## Bài 14.2 — ことができます

<div class="quiz-section">

<div class="quiz-question" data-correct="a">
<p class="question-text">"Tôi có thể lái xe." = ?</p>
<div class="question-options">
<button class="option-btn" data-value="a" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'a')"><span lang="ja">車を運転することができます。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="b" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'a')"><span lang="ja">車を運転してことができます。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="c" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'a')"><span lang="ja">車を運転したことができます。</span></button>
</div>
<div class="question-explanation" style="display:none;">
ことができます dùng <strong>dictionary form</strong>: <span lang="ja">運転する</span>ことができます.
</div>
</div>

<div class="quiz-question" data-correct="b">
<p class="question-text">"Bạn có thể chơi piano không?" = ?</p>
<div class="question-options">
<button class="option-btn" data-value="a" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'b')"><span lang="ja">ピアノを弾いたことがありますか？</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="b" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'b')"><span lang="ja">ピアノを弾くことができますか？</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="c" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'b')"><span lang="ja">ピアノを弾いてことができますか？</span></button>
</div>
<div class="question-explanation" style="display:none;">
"Có thể" = ことができますか. Đáp án A là mẫu "đã từng" (たことがある), không phải "có thể".
</div>
</div>

<div class="quiz-question" data-correct="c">
<p class="question-text">"Tôi không thể bơi." = ?</p>
<div class="question-options">
<button class="option-btn" data-value="a" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'c')"><span lang="ja">泳ぐことができます。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="b" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'c')"><span lang="ja">泳いだことがありません。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="c" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'c')"><span lang="ja">泳ぐことができません。</span></button>
</div>
<div class="question-explanation" style="display:none;">
"Không thể" = ことが<strong>できません</strong>. <span lang="ja">泳ぐ</span>ことができません.
</div>
</div>

<div class="quiz-question" data-correct="a">
<p class="question-text">"Tôi có thể nấu ăn." = ?</p>
<div class="question-options">
<button class="option-btn" data-value="a" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'a')"><span lang="ja">料理を作ることができます。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="b" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'a')"><span lang="ja">料理を作ってことができます。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="c" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'a')"><span lang="ja">料理を作りことができます。</span></button>
</div>
<div class="question-explanation" style="display:none;">
<span lang="ja">作る</span> (tsukuru) là dictionary form. <span lang="ja">作る</span>ことができます = "có thể làm/nấu".
</div>
</div>

## Bài 14.3 — V前に

<div class="quiz-section">

<div class="quiz-question" data-correct="b">
<p class="question-text">"Trước khi đi Nhật, tôi đã học tiếng Nhật." = ?</p>
<div class="question-options">
<button class="option-btn" data-value="a" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'b')"><span lang="ja">日本に行った前に日本語を勉強しました。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="b" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'b')"><span lang="ja">日本に行く前に日本語を勉強しました。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="c" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'b')"><span lang="ja">日本に行きます前に日本語を勉強しました。</span></button>

<div class="question-explanation" style="display:none;">
V前に luôn dùng <strong>dictionary form</strong>, dù câu chính ở quá khứ: <span lang="ja">行く</span>前に.
</div>
</div>

<div class="quiz-question" data-correct="a">
<p class="question-text">"Trước khi ăn cơm, tôi nói いただきます." = ?</p>
<div class="question-options">
<button class="option-btn" data-value="a" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'a')"><span lang="ja">ご飯を食べる前に「いただきます」と言います。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="b" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'a')"><span lang="ja">ご飯を食べて前に「いただきます」と言います。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="c" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'a')"><span lang="ja">ご飯を食べた前に「いただきます」と言います。</span></button>
</div>
<div class="question-explanation" style="display:none;">
<span lang="ja">食べる</span>前に = trước khi ăn. Dictionary form trước <span lang="ja">前に</span>.
</div>
</div>

<div class="quiz-question" data-correct="c">
<p class="question-text">"Trước khi ngủ, tôi đọc sách." = ?</p>
<div class="question-options">
<button class="option-btn" data-value="a" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'c')"><span lang="ja">寝ます前に本を読みます。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="b" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'c')"><span lang="ja">寝た前に本を読みます。</span></button>
<button class="option-btn" data-value="c" onclick="LJQuiz.checkAnswer(this, 'c')"><span lang="ja">寝る前に本を読みます。</span></button>
</div>
<div class="question-explanation" style="display:none;">
<span lang="ja">寝る</span>前に = trước khi ngủ. Luôn dictionary form!
